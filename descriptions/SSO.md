# Scope

This **Ship Security Officer (SSO)** course covers the mandatory competences and the required knowledge, understanding and proficiencies prescribed in Section A VI/5, Table A-VI/5 of the STCW Code.

This requires a total of **twenty-four (24)** instructional hours and **four (4)** assessment hours to cover the course topics.

# Entry Standards

Entry to the course is open to all officers duly designated as Ship Security Officer (SSO) by the shipping company and shipping management.

They must be medically fit to enable them to participate in all aspects of the training particularly the practical exercises.

# Course Certificate

On successful completion of the requirements of the course, a **Certificate of Training Completion** shall be issued certifying a holder's acquisition of the mandatory minimum requirements and competence as specified in Regulation VI/5 of the STCW Convention, 1978, as amended.
