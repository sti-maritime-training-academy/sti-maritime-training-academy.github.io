# Scope

The Maritime Cybersecurity Awareness covers specific competencies to provide trainees with basic knowledge, skills, and best practices necessary to secure maritime systems, networks, and data cyberthreats including handle of social media against cyberthreats. The program aims to train the shipâ€™s crew and company personnel on how to identify risks and the methods to prepare effective measures on-board and ashore against cyber threats and incidents.

This requires a total of **eight (8)** instructional hours and **one (1)** assessment hour to cover the course topics.

# Entry Standards

Entry on the course is open to all trainees who are medically fit to enable them to participate in all aspects of the training, particularly the practical exercises.

# Course Certificate

Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a **Certificate of Training Completion**.
