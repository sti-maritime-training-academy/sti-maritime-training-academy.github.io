# Scope

The **2nd Mate Familiarization** course provides participants with essential knowledge and practical skills to perform the duties of a 2nd on board a vessel required to assume the responsibilities of a 2nd Mate or Junior Officer aboard a vessel. The course intends to develop an officer’s capabilities in passage planning, voyage reporting, bridge resource management, medical chest management, and general safety.

The course is designed to help a first time second mate to conduct his first voyage as a navigating officer. A total of 32 instructional hours, including assessment, are required to complete the course.

# Training Outcomes

This course is designed for marine officers newly assigned or about to assume the role of 2nd Mate or Navigation Officer.

Specifically, at the end of the course the trainee must be able to:

- Understand navigation concepts essential in preparing a passage plan.
- Determine the duties of a second mate according to industry practice as well as company specific requirements.
- Familiarize, check and maintain navigational equipment.
- Develop a quick reference guide for passage planning in line with the vessel’s operation manual and navigation policy. 
- Prepare a passage plan in line with the voyage order in coordination with the master considering the standard stages of passage planning (Appraisal, Planning, Execution, Monitoring)
- Facilitate a pre-departure meeting discussing the peculiarities of the voyage to the bridge team.
- Be able to revise passage plan based on latest information.
- Organize the ship’s hospital ensuring the validity of all medicines and medical equipment.
- Identify the various references and software that can assist a second mate.
- Safe Navigational watch on understanding UKC, Squat, ECDIS setting Safety depth parameters, importance of CATZOC in Navigation
- Understanding of IALA Buoyage

# Entry Standards

The course is open to deck seafarer’s with OIC-NW license and has been a part of the bridge team in various stages of navigation.

# Course Certificate

Upon successful completion of the course, participants will receive a **Certificate of Training Completion**. This certifies their attendance and competence in performing the duties of a 2nd Officer as required by maritime standards.
